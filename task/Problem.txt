some gnomes were equipped with 1st generation tools, others with 2nd generation.
After the introduction of new generation tooling Gnome Inc CEO noticed that performance didn’t match his expectations.
You as an analyst of Gnome Inc should confirm or reject CEO worries supporting your findings by cold hard data.
